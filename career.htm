Positions Available

GIS Specialist

Regular Full-Time
Key Accountabilities
  • Responsible for development, implementation and maintenance of GIS project applications.
  • Liaises with municipal staff to determine user needs and offers solutions to user identified GIS problems.
  • Ensures optimization of the GIS platform and technological tools.
  • Oversees installation and implementation of new GIS hardware and software.
  • Maintains the geographic information system for City departments, enters and edits graphic and non-graphic data from source maps and related documentation in a timely manner to support operational and strategic decision making; produces hard copy maps and reports as requested by departments and for the public.
Qualifications
  • A minimum of five years’ direct experience in professional GIS applications, managing infrastructure and land-related data, preferably in a local government environment.
  • Membership or eligibility for membership in the Applied Science Technologists and Technicians of Ontario as an Applied Science Technologist.
  • Industry certificates in relevant specialties are desirable.
  • Possession of a valid Ontario Driver’s License with a good driving record.
Salary and Hours of Work

The salary is $40.11 per hour based on a 35 hour work week in accordance with the current Collective Agreement.

GIS analyst

Regular Full-Time
Key Accountabilities
  • Organize, coordinate and assist in land and resource development projects that utilize GIS.
  • Efficiently perform GIS analysis in the delivery of land management projects.
  • Present data on pdf and web maps using ArcGIS Server JavaScript API and ArcGIS Web AppBuilder with the ability to develop custom widgets.
  • Support in house and client use of ArcMap Collector.
  • Documentation, quality control, and incremental improvement over time.
Qualifications
  • Post-secondary education with a degree or diploma in a relevant discipline.
  • Minimum of 5 years of directly related experience.
  • Proficient in ESRI ArcGIS tools and integration with Python for efficiency and repeatability.
  • Excellent problem solving and analytical abilities (analytics using math/basic statistics will be required).
  • Broad understanding and implementation of land management concepts and practices.
  • A professional, friendly and courteous manner (service & team oriented).
Ideal Candidate
  • Experience using ArcGIS Server and web mapping tools such as the JavaScript API and Web AppBuilder
  • Use of Pandas in Python for analysis and reporting
  • Problem solving skills, initiative, and an intuitive ability.
  • Strong organisational skills and technical writing skills.
  • Self-direction, flexibility and an ability to work under limited direct supervision.
  • Good technical understanding of databases and Microsoft Office™ applications.
Salary and Hours of Work

The salary is $40.11 per hour based on a 35 hour work week in accordance with the current Collective Agreement.
