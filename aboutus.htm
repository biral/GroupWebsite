Established in 2018, Nutshell GIS Consulting provides high-quality environmental services for private and public sector clients in Canada and throughout the world. Nutshell provides services in the areas of ecology, environmental assessment and monitoring, contaminant monitoring, GIS and remote sensing, biodiversity assessments, GIS programming, geological analysis, spatial analysis, and cartography.


We specialize in undertaking complex, multi-disciplinary environmental projects, often in collaboration with networks of local experts and specialists in particular fields. Our work centers on providing cost-effective, sustainable solutions to our clients’ environmental needs.


Our office is in Lindsay, Ontario, Canada. Our consultants are graduates from GIS Application or Cartography Specialist Post-grad Program. We are professionals with over 5 years of experience on GIS. Our long-standing commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a company that meets all your needs and fulfill your expectations. Our staff is dedicated to the firm's goal of 100% satisfaction in every project we undertake.
